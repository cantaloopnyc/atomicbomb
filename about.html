The Man Behind the Cam lurks in the furthest corners of arenas, tapings and signings,
seen by no-one, yet seeing everything.

Some say he is a forgotten wrestling star.
Others say he was just a contender who couldn't cut it in the ring.

Or maybe he's just a guy raised on bootleg wrestling tapes,
living in his parents' basement
and practicing Atomic Bombs off the sofa.

Whoever the Man Behind the Cam is,
he travels far and wide...

...photographing pay-per-views...

...documenting DDTs and death drops...

...forever capturing the greatness of these superstars in spandex.
